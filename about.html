About Moonbeam

Moonbeam is a hybrid 2D/3D arcade game where the player controls Verne, a TV-headed robot, on missions to collect currency in a procedurally generated cosmic environment. The core gameplay centers on a unique movement mechanic: using "Gravity Nodes" to pull Verne into orbit and slingshot him through space while avoiding asteroids and black holes. The game features a risk/reward system based on a depleting fuel gauge and limited health.

The main objective is twofold: master the movement mechanics to gather Stardust and rare Moonbeams, and use that collected currency to create and customize constellations in a separate, creative game mode. The experience is tied together with a retro-futuristic aesthetic, cute and soft 3D shapes, collectible minigames, and a diegetic radio that the player can control at any time.

Described by playtesters as "Katamari core" and "a Mario Galaxy mini arcade", we at Team Moonbeam hope you make some space in your future to check out Moonbeam's many unlockable minigames, kitchy easter egg collectibles, interactive radio and relaxing gameplay. Stay tuned for 3D art updates, fun extras, and insights into Moonbeam's development!
